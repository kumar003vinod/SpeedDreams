Sports car model LS1 Archer R9 (Aston Martin DBR9 2005)
--

Loosely based on an Aston Martin DBR9 model by Lukas and Lubos of Czech SCGT.
Ported to Torcs, driver/interior and numerous other enhancements added by
Andrew Sumner, 2007.

Usage of the Eclipse logo with permission from Barb Cochrane, Eclipse Foundation, Inc.
